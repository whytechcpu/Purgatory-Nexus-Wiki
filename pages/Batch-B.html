Batch B

Batch B is the second batch made for Purgatory Nexus, containing 6 characters total.

Batch B started creation on July 3rd 2024 and ended on August 29th 2024.
After Takmit was created (July 7th 2024), the character creation stopped for a while, and revived itself after Bleerpi was created.
Horn Cube was made way before Purgatory Nexus, but was put in Batch-B just so it can have 6 characters instead of 5.
